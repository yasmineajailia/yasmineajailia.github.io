NEXTLEARN

NEXTLEARN

Course activity→who needs help, and why

An e-learning platform for Esprit's C programming course that watches how students actually work and intervenes before they fall behind. A model forecasts dropout risk and exam performance; SHAP explains every prediction, so a tutor sees the reasoning rather than a bare score. Alongside it: a RAG chatbot answering only from course material, VARK-based learning-style recommendations, and webcam attention tracking that runs entirely in the browser — no images ever leave the student's device.
